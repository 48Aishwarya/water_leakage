# Welcome to your water_leakage project

The Water Leakage Detection and Municipal Alert System is a smart solution designed to identify water leaks in pipelines and automatically notify municipal authorities. This system helps in reducing water wastage, preventing damage, and improving efficient water management in cities and towns.

The system uses sensors to continuously monitor water flow and pressure in pipelines. When a leakage is detected (due to abnormal flow or pressure drop), the system immediately sends an alert to the municipal department through a communication module.
